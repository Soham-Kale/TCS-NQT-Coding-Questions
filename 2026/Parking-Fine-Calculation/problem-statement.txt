Q1 — Parking Fine Calculation
Problem:

A parking system calculates fines based on hours parked. Given integer hours, determine the correct fine.

Fine Slabs:
Short stay
hours ≤ 2
₹100

Medium stay
2 < hours ≤ 5
₹50

Long stay
hours > 5
₹20
Input:

Single integer — hours

Output:

Integer fine (100, 50, or 20)


Test Case 1

Input: hours = 2

Condition: hours ≤ 2

Boundary case — exactly 2 hrs hits first condition

Fine Output:
100

Test Case 2

Input: hours = 4

Condition: 2 < hours ≤ 5

4 > 2 and 4 ≤ 5 — falls in middle slab

Fine Output:
50

Test Case 3

Input: hours = 6

Condition: hours > 5

6 > 5 — falls in last else block

Fine Output:
20
