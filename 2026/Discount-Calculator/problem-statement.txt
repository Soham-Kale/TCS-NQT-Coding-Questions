Q1 — Discount Calculator
Problem:

Given a purchase amount, apply a discount based on slabs and print the final payable 
amount rounded to 2 decimal places.

Discount Slabs:
5% discount
amount < 1000
10% discount
1000 ≤ amount < 5000
15% discount
amount ≥ 5000
Input:

Single integer — the purchase amount

Output:

Final amount after discount, printed to 2 decimal places (e.g., "760.00")

Formula:

Final Amount = Amount − (Amount × Discount Rate)


Q1 — Test Cases — Dry Run
Test Case 1

Input: 800

Slab: < 1000 → 5%
Discount: 5% of 800 = 40

Final Amount:
800 − 40 = 760.00

Test Case 2

Input: 2000

Slab: 1000–5000 → 10%
Discount: 10% of 2000 = 200

Final Amount:
2000 − 200 = 1800.00

Test Case 3

Input: 6000

Slab: ≥ 5000 → 15%
Discount: 15% of 6000 = 900

Final Amount:
6000 − 900 = 5100.00
