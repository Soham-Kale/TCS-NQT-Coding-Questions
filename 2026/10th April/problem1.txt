✅ **Question 1: Student Marks Calculation (2D Array)**

📄 **Problem Statement**

You are given the marks of students in two exams: **Internal** and **External**.

• Marks are stored in two separate **2D matrices** of size R × C.
• Each element represents marks of a student.
• You are given an index (i, j) (0-based indexing).

👉 **Task:**
Calculate the **total marks** of the student at position (i, j).


🔍 Example

Input:
2 2
10 20
30 40
15 25
35 45
1 1

Output:
85
